FIVE OAKS MUSIC PACK

This uses the ten real WAV singles Lori uploaded and converts them to MP3 for the app.

Songs included:
- Five Oaks
- Five Oaks Calling
- Lantern Light Between Us
- Little Prairie Fire
- The Day I Found You
- The Long Shot
- The Long Way Forward
- The Night Before Tomorrow
- The Woman They Couldn't Ignore
- Under The Five Oaks Sky

Upload steps:
1. In GitHub, open five-oaks-app.
2. Upload the whole music folder.
3. Upload music.css and music.js to the main/root folder beside index.html.
4. Edit index.html.
5. Replace the current placeholder Music section with music-section-replacement.html.
6. Add <link rel="stylesheet" href="music.css"> before </head>.
7. Add <script src="music.js"></script> before </body>.
8. Commit changes. GitHub Pages will update the app.
