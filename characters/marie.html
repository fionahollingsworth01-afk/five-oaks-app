Marie Elizabeth Simpson Callahan Masters

Marie is the emotional heart of the Callahan family. She was married to Roger Callahan and later to Bob Masters.

She loved and raised Jace as one of her own, and she is mother to Krys, Rance, Royce, and Rhys.

Visual references: Marie in the later books, and Roger and Marie with Krys, Rance, Royce, and Rhys.
