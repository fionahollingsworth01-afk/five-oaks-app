Biography

Roger Callahan is not a gentle root in the family tree. He is father to Jace from his first marriage and father to Krys, Rance, Royce, and Rhys with Marie. His choices leave marks on the children who carry his name, especially in the way they fight to become something better than what hurt them.

The Weight of the Callahan Name

Roger’s presence reaches far beyond one room. The Callahan children spend their lives carrying, rejecting, surviving, or answering what he left behind.
