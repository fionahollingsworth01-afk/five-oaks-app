Biography

Krys is one of the original forces at the heart of Five Oaks. She was shaped by the Callahan family, tested by violence, and became part of the bond that turned dangerous men and hard choices into a permanent home. She is not decoration in this family. She is one of its builders.

Her marriage to Jake holds through the long years. Together they have two sons, Kai and Wes, and her place in Five Oaks remains central across the original saga and the next generation.

Relationships

Jake Kincaid: husband and lifelong partner.

Matt Haskins: one of the deepest bonds in the saga, built on trust, protection, and a history neither of them carries lightly.

Luke Rawlins and Jace Callahan: part of the original Five Oaks family structure and equal partnership.

Kai and Wes: her sons with Jake.
