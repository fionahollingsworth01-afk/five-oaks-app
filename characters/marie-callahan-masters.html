Marie Elizabeth Simpson Callahan Masters

Role: The heart of the Callahan family and one of the emotional foundations of Five Oaks.

Basic Information

  • Maiden Name: Simpson
  • First Husband: Roger Callahan
  • Second Husband: Bob Masters
  • Age: 65 during Kai's book

Children

  • Jace Callahan — Roger's son from his first marriage; Marie loved and raised him as one of her own.
  • Krysten “Krys” Callahan Kincaid
  • Rance Callahan
  • Royce Callahan
  • Rhys Callahan — the baby of the Callahan family

Birth Order

  • Jace
  • Three years later: Krys
  • Two years later: Rance
  • Two years later: Royce
  • Two years later: Rhys

Physical Description

Marie has strawberry-blonde hair in youth, gradually becoming silver-reddish blonde. Her eyes are blue-gray, and her build is strong from years of ranch work. She carries warmth, quiet confidence, and gentle strength.

Personality

Marie is compassionate, patient, resilient, and quietly strong. She welcomes people easily, calms frightened children, comforts grieving adults, and protects her family with steady resolve.

Background

Marie married Roger Callahan and together they raised the Callahan children. Though Jace was not her biological son, she never treated him differently from Krys, Rance, Royce, or Rhys. After Roger's death, Bob Masters brings companionship and happiness back into her life.

Legacy

Marie represents home. She is the heart of the Callahan family and one of the foundations upon which Five Oaks is built.
