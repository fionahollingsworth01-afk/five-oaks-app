Roger Callahan

Role: Callahan patriarch, father, husband, and complicated root of the family line.

Basic Information

  • First Wife: Margaret Anne St. Clair Callahan
  • Second Wife: Marie Elizabeth Simpson Callahan Masters
  • Age: 56 when he died

Children

  • Jace Callahan — Roger's son with Margaret Anne St. Clair Callahan
  • Krysten “Krys” Callahan Kincaid — Roger and Marie's first child together
  • Rance Callahan
  • Royce Callahan
  • Rhys Callahan — the baby of the Callahan family

Birth Order

  • Jace
  • Three years later: Krys
  • Two years later: Rance
  • Two years later: Royce
  • Two years later: Rhys

Physical Description

Roger is a strong, weathered man shaped by land, authority, and years of work. He carries the posture of a man used to being obeyed and the seriousness of someone who believes order matters more than comfort.

Personality

Roger is proud, strict, stubborn, and often hard to reach emotionally. He loves his family, but his need for order and respectability often wounds the people he means to guide.

Background

Roger was a widower with a young son when he met Marie Simpson. He later built a large Callahan family with her, but his relationships with his children became complicated by pressure, expectations, and his belief that love required correction.

Legacy

Roger's legacy is complicated. He gives the Callahan family its roots, but those roots are tangled with both love and harm. His children carry pieces of him forward, each deciding in their own way what to keep and what to refuse.
