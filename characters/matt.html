Biography

Matt Haskins is the planner, the counter, the man who reads danger before most people admit it exists. He keeps the books, watches the edges, and protects the family in ways that often cost him more than anyone sees.

The Man Who Counts the Cost

Matt’s strength is not noise. It is calculation, loyalty, and the kind of love that does not ask to be noticed before it sacrifices.

His past with Clara leaves wounds that never fully disappear. His touch aversion and spells are part of what he carries, but they do not define all he is. Five Oaks gives him family, and he gives that family everything he can.

Relationships

Krys Callahan Kincaid

Krys is Matt’s chosen sister, his anchor, and one of the few people who can read the pain he tries to hide.

Jake Kincaid

Jake and Matt trust each other with the old hard loyalty of men who have stood through danger together.

Luke Rawlins

Luke brings warmth, food, humor, and family into places Matt would otherwise close off.

Jace Callahan

Jace is part of the Five Oaks partnership and the Callahan family tie that helps bind the ranch together.

Timeline Notes

Before Five Oaks: Matt becomes part of the brotherhood formed before the ranch exists.

Clara: he loses Clara, and that grief shapes him for the rest of his life.

Early Five Oaks: he helps build the ranch with Krys, Jake, Luke, and Jace.

Later Five Oaks: Matt remains the ledger-keeper, strategist, and one of the people the family depends on when trouble comes.

Inheritance: Matt’s share passes to Jennifer.
