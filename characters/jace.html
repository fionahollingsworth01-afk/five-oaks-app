Biography

Jace Callahan is one of the original Five Oaks partners and one of the Callahan anchors of the saga. He is tied to Krys through family, but the life they build turns that tie into something far stronger than paperwork ever could.

Callahan Blood and Five Oaks Grit

Jace carries the Callahan name into the next generation through Eli, Cole, Sawyer, Dawson, Jennifer, and Caleb, even as Five Oaks teaches him that family is also what people keep choosing under pressure.

Relationships

Krys Callahan Kincaid

Jace is Krys’ brother through family. Whatever began through their parents’ marriage became a lifelong bond at Five Oaks.

Susanna Pike

Susanna remains Pike, and their children carry the Callahan name. Their bond is complicated, lasting, and woven through the next generation.

Jake, Matt, and Luke

Jace stands with the other Five Oaks men as an equal partner in the ranch and the family they built.

Timeline Notes

Before Five Oaks: Jace becomes part of the hard road that leads the men toward the ranch.

Early Five Oaks: he helps build the ranch with Krys, Jake, Matt, and Luke.

Family: Jace and Susanna have Eli, Cole, Sawyer, Dawson, Jennifer, and Caleb.

Later Years: Jace’s acceptance of Tate helps prepare the family to defend Jennifer, Waya, and Tsula.
