Jace Callahan

Role: Truth, pressure, family loyalty, and one of the Five Oaks of Five Oaks.

Basic Information

  • Father: Roger Callahan
  • Mother: Margaret Anne St. Clair Callahan
  • Stepmother: Marie Elizabeth Simpson Callahan Masters
  • Birth Year: 1866
  • Companion: Susanna Pike
  • Children: Eli, Cole, Sawyer, Dawson, Jennifer, and Caleb Callahan

Appearance

Jace has dark blond to light brown hair, hazel eyes, and a lean, wiry build. He can look younger than he is until the smile drops and anger sharpens his face.

At His Core

Jace is not a planner. He is a reader of pressure. He knows when someone is bluffing, when a room is turning, and when it is time to leave before trouble takes over. He survives by moving first.

Family

Jace and Roger never fit together easily. Roger believed in order, obedience, respectability, and keeping problems contained. Jace was the problem that would not stay contained. Marie loved and raised him as one of her own, and Krys never thought of him as anything but her brother.

Krys

Jace and Krys have always been tight. Not loud affection. Not showy protection. Just loyalty without question. He taught her how to read people, how to leave a room when it turns, and how to stand in herself. She grounded him. He shielded her when he could.

The Five Oaks

Jace belongs among the five because he refuses the kind of peace that depends on silence. He is there because truth matters, and Five Oaks cannot stand if it is built on avoidance.

Legacy

Jace’s legacy is loyalty that can survive truth. He tests the people who love him, but he also strengthens them because he will not let family become another word for control.
