Biography

Jake Kincaid is one of the men who becomes part of the original Five Oaks bond before the ranch has fully become home. He is not loud with his loyalty, and he does not waste words proving what his actions already show. When Jake stands with someone, he stands all the way.

A Man Built on Restraint

Jake is quiet, controlled, private, and deeply protective. He notices what has to be done and does it before most people finish talking.

At Five Oaks, Jake becomes husband to Krys and father to Kai and Wes. His life is tied to the ranch, the men who built it with him, and the family that grows from the choices all five of them made together.

Jake and Krys

Jake’s marriage to Krys is one of the central roots of Five Oaks. Their sons, Kai and Wes, carry the Kincaid line into the next generation.

Relationships

Krys Callahan Kincaid

Krys is Jake’s wife, partner, and the woman who becomes home in every way that matters.

Matt Haskins

Jake and Matt trust each other in the old, hard way of men who have seen danger together and know the other will not run.

Luke Rawlins

Luke becomes the brother Jake did not know he needed, bringing warmth and humor into places Jake would rather keep guarded.

Jace Callahan

Jace is family through Krys. What began through marriage became a lifelong Callahan-Kincaid bond tied into the foundation of Five Oaks.

Timeline Notes

Before Five Oaks: Jake becomes part of the brotherhood that forms before the ranch is a settled home.

Early Five Oaks: he helps build the ranch with Krys, Matt, Luke, and Jace.

Marriage: Jake marries Krys and builds a family with her.

Next Generation: he and Krys raise Kai and Wes, and his share of Five Oaks is meant for Wes.

1916: Jake is fifty during Eli’s book and remains one of the central old-guard figures holding the family together.
