Biography

Luke Rawlins is one of the original Five Oaks partners, a man who learned early how much cruelty could take and then spent his life proving it did not get to take everything. He uses humor, food, work, and warmth to reach people who might otherwise stay locked inside themselves.

Built for More

Luke becomes one of the builders of Five Oaks in every sense: cabins, tables, meals, trust, and the kind of home people return to when the world has worn them down.

Family

  • Emma Rawlins — wife

Relationships

Emma Rawlins

Emma sees Luke as more than the damage he survived, and loving her becomes part of how he learns to believe he was built for more.

Krys Callahan Kincaid

Krys gives Luke family without making him perform for his place at the table.

Matt Haskins

Luke brings warmth into places Matt would rather guard, and Matt trusts him because the care is real.

Timeline Notes

Before Five Oaks: Luke leaves home young and carries wounds from what was done to him.

Early Five Oaks: he helps build the ranch with Krys, Jake, Matt, and Jace.

Marriage: Luke marries Emma and builds a family with her.

Later Years: Luke remains one of the old-guard anchors of Five Oaks.
