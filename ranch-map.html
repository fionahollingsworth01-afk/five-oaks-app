Five Oaks Ranch Map

The illustrated ranch map: Five Oaks, the east fence, the small fishing creek, Patterson property, the future oil field, and Waya and Tsula’s hillside campsite.

Map canon: Patterson sits east of the Five Oaks fence line. The small fishing creek belongs to Five Oaks. The Patterson farm is the future oil field. Waya and Tsula’s campsite is on the hillside beyond the Patterson place, where they can look west over the old farmhouse, barn, chicken houses, and toward Five Oaks.
